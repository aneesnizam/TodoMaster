How TodoMaster Works

Get organized in just a few simple steps

1

Create Your Account

Sign up in less than a minute to start organizing your tasks. Just provide your email and create a password.

2

Add Your Tasks

Click the "Add Task" button and enter your task details. You can add a title, description, due date, and category.

3

Organize & Prioritize

Use categories to organize your tasks and star important ones. Filter tasks by status (active/completed) to focus on what matters.

4

Stay Productive

Check off completed tasks, set reminders, and enjoy the satisfaction of getting things done with our clean, distraction-free interface.
